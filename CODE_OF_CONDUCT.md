# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in our community a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or
sexual identity and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Other conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the maintainers via a GitHub
Security Advisory or a private message. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1.
